Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a popular Indian dish made with tender chicken pieces cooked in a creamy tomato-based sauce. It is known for its rich and flavorful taste.

Ingredients
  • Chicken
  • Butter
  • Tomatoes
  • Cream
  • Spices
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Onions
  • Cashew nuts
  • Yogurt
Biryani

Biryani is a flavorful Indian rice dish made with aromatic spices and meat or vegetables. It is known for its rich and fragrant taste.

Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Meat or Vegetables
  • Spices
  • Onions
  • Ghee
  • Yogurt
  • Mint leaves
Paneer Tikka

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian dish made with marinated paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and grilled to perfection. It is known for its smoky and flavorful taste.

Ingredients
  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Yogurt
  • Spices
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Lemon juice
  • Oil
